HOW TO PLAY
THE DONALD: A PRESIDENTIAL SIMULATOR
🎯 THE GOAL
You are Donald Trump. You have two ways to win: survive all 4 terms (you'll find out how it ends), OR achieve total world domination by annexing every country on the map. There is one way to lose: accidentally Make America Great Again — if your MAGA% hits 100, you lose.
📊 YOUR STATS
Approval — How much America likes you. Mostly irrelevant.
Economy — How the markets are doing. You will ruin this.
World Relations — How much the world hates you.
Legal Trouble — Indictments piling up. Ignore them.
Chaos — Higher = more unhinged. Keep it moderate.
🇺🇸 MAGA% — YOUR DEATH METER. Do NOT let this hit 100.
📋 MAKING DECISIONS
Each turn you get a situation and up to 4 choices. Your decision history scrolls — you can see everything you've done. Choices are color-coded:
🔥 VERY TRUMP — Maximum chaos. High risk, raises Legal and Chaos. Lowers MAGA (good!)
📌 CLASSIC TRUMP — Standard controversial moves. Balanced chaos.
✅ REASONABLE — Actually good policy. DANGER: raises MAGA significantly.
❌ NOT TRUMP — Things he'd never do. Often raises MAGA the most.
🌍 WORLD DOMINATION PATH
Click any country on the World Map tab to open diplomacy options. To annex a country: 1) Sanction or Deploy Troops to worsen relations → 2) Escalate to Hostile → 3) Airstrike or MOAB to start War → 4) Build military strength with more troops → 5) When the war bar shows you're winning, the ANNEX NOW button appears. Annex every country to win. The war panel shows live battle odds and quick-action buttons.
🗳️ ELECTIONS & TERMS
Around Day 730 of each term, an election interrupts gameplay. Pick 3 campaign promises — the more unhinged the better. Low chaos = lose the election (and the game). After Term 2, you must find a way to bypass the 22nd Amendment. After Term 3, bypass it again. Survive all 4 terms and the ending is considered a "win". Every restart randomizes world relations.
🚔 ICE TAB    🐦 TWEETS TAB    📰 LOG TAB
The ICE tab lets you run deportation operations anytime — great for raising Chaos fast. The Tweets tab tracks everything you've tweeted. The Log tab is your full decision history.
